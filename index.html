Little Universe Academy

Little Universe Academy is a wide-ranging training center with enrichment in languages, music, art, robotics, and more. Our aim is to provide children with a happy and integrated learning environment. The founders are all front-line educators who have career-long constant teaching engagement. Each course is designated to extend children's potential and cultivate children's imagination and creativity. Our criteria for teachers are: professional, devoted, and passionate.
