  • Sensei Noah teaches self-awareness and basic self-protection in a fun, age-appropriate way

Additional notes:

  • Uniform fee: $45
  • Families may purchase a uniform, bring their own, or wear regular athletic clothing
  • Uniform fees are paid directly to Sensei Noah

2. NEW Course: Young Musicians Ensemble

We are excited to introduce a children's ensemble program, where kids learn music through joyful collaboration.

Program elements include:

  • Two-piano, four-hand playing to build rhythm and harmony
  • Violin and other melodic instruments (children may bring their own instrument)
  • Chorus training to strengthen pitch, listening, and musical memory
  • Percussion (kids' version) activities to experience rhythm through movement

Learning goals:

  • Maintain steady rhythm and listen to others
  • Express themselves musically, not just “play notes”
  • Take responsibility within a group and enjoy teamwork

IX. Selected Teacher Profiles

(For more details, please visit the partner websites above.)

1. Director: Yan Feng

Education:

  • Master of Human Development and Psychology, Harvard Graduate School of Education
  • Master of American Literature

Ms. Feng is a certified teacher in both China and the United States, with extensive experience in Reggio-inspired, Montessori, and Free Play educational approaches. As the founder of Little Universe Academy (LUA), she is deeply committed to creating meaningful and joyful learning experiences for children.

2. Karate Instructor: Noah Lucia

Noah began Uechi-Ryu karate training at age 3 and grew up in his father's dojo. He also trained in judo and played ice hockey at Norwich University. After graduating in 2008, he traveled to Japan to teach English and study Japanese Kempo. He now continues the family tradition, with his own daughters training under their grandfather, Papa Sensei.

3. Public Speaking & Debate: Stuart Pink

Education:

  • Law Degree, University of Oxford
  • Education Degree, Canterbury Christ Church University

Highlights:

  • Six-time District 31 International Speech Contest Champion
  • 3rd place at Toastmasters International Convention (30,000+ competitors)
  • Former Chair, New England Chapter, National Speakers Association

Stuart believes public speaking is a critical life skill that can be learned—and enjoyed—by everyone.

4. Chorus Instructor: Xuanhao Li

Education:

  • Master of Music, Longy School of Music

An accomplished soprano with extensive performance and teaching experience, Ms. Li has trained under renowned international vocal masters and has won multiple international awards. She has over ten years of teaching experience and prepares students for ABRSM exams and recitals.
